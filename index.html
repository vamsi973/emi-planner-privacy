EMI Planner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1 Introduction

EMI Planner ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free loan EMI calculator app built for Indian borrowers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2 Data We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

EMI Planner works completely offline. All data you enter (loan amount, interest rate, tenure, saved plans) is stored only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server or third party.

We absolutely do not collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Financial account information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

3 Local Storage

Saved loan plans are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is only accessible within the app and is deleted when you uninstall the app. We have no access to this data.

4 Camera Permission

The app declares a camera permission in its manifest for potential future document scanning features. This permission is not currently used. The app will never access your camera without your explicit action.

5 Third-Party Services

EMI Planner does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6 Children's Privacy

EMI Planner does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features that target children.

7 Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend keeping your device locked with a PIN or biometric lock.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
